Why DocPlan?


Many clinics and hospitals still rely on manual appointment books or WhatsApp communication to coordinate doctor visits. DocPlan offers a digital solution that organizes appointments, minimizes missed visits, and reduces waiting room congestion.
Our aim is to make healthcare faster and more accessible by empowering patients to connect with doctors and emergencies to be handled more efficiently.

Who is it for?


Doctors:View appointments, get to see which to approve or to cancel, receive emergency alerts
and in the process reduce administrative work therefore improving engagement with patients.
Patients: Easily book appointments, get reminders, receivetimely notifications to ensure preparedness
and declare emergencies at their convinience without waiting for office hours.
