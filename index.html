Your health, your schedule, your way. Book appointments, get reminders, and more!

We bring to you...

DocPlan is a modern healthcare mobile app solution that connects patients and doctors instantly with just one tap of a button on your phone or any other device. Whether it is booking or schedulingappointments, declaring emergencies, DocPlan puts healthcare in your hands anytime, anywhere. DocPlan is here for all your healthcare needs.

Why DocPlan?


Many clinics and hospitals have been relying on manual appointment books, WhatsApp communication and even trekking
to the hospitals in order to book or see a doctor, which is tiresome and waste of time and effort especially when the doctor is a no show.
DocPlan offers a digital solution that organizes appointments, minimizes missed visits, and reduces waiting room congestion.
Our aim is to make healthcare faster, more efficient and more accessible by empowering patients to connect with doctors
and emergencies to be handled more efficiently.

Who is it for?


Doctors:They get to use the app to view appointments, see which to approve or to decline, receive emergency alerts and reminders of upcoming appointments.
and in the process reduce administrative work therefore improving interractions with patients which in term improves doctor-patient relationship.

Patients: Book appointments easily without the hustle of going to the hospital physically, get reminders, receive timely notifications to ensure preparedness
and declare emergencies at their convinience without waiting for office hours.
